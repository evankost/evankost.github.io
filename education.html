EDUCATION

In addition to my focus on IT certifications and seminar attendance, my educational foundation lies in mathematics. I am a graduate of the Department of Mathematics at UOA, specializing in computational mathematics, which includes both theoretical and practical computer science. Despite the mixed feelings towards mathematics, particularly among computer scientists, I enjoy discovering the underlying connections between these two sciences.

Mathematics is composed of various branches, each focusing on different aspects and applications. Algebra (personal favorite) explores structures and equations, while analysis deals with functions and limits. Geometry studies properties of spaces, and probability/statistics analyze randomness and data. Discrete mathematics handles countable objects, and computational mathematics uses computers to solve mathematical problems.
